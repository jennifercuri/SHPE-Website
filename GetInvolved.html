For High School and Middle School Students

Dia de Ciencias

Dia de Ciencias is a series of events that are geared towards inspiring and engaging minorities to pursue a degree and career in STEM. The goal of this program is to increase awareness of STEM fields and careers and increase students' belief in their ability to enter and succeed in the STEM field. The activities, networking opportunities, and information session about SHPE, as well as the parent workshop are all in place to help make students and parents aware of the multitude of opportunities around them.


Please contact outreach@rushpe.org for more information

The Shadow Program

This is a two day FREE program for students in their sophomore through senior year of high school! Within the two days, this program will expose the many paths and opportunities within the world of engineering through workshops and immersive activities!

Please contact communityservice@rushpe.org for more information
